# Experiment — Personality Content as Matching Evidence

| Field | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Status | Designed, not started |
| Related Epic | [Personality Layer](../epics/EPIC-04_PERSONALITY_LAYER.md) |
| Related issue | #95 |

## Hypothesis

Optional structured prompts and a small member-selected set of stories will help people understand
who someone is beyond credentials, improve shortlist confidence and conversation specificity, and
increase replies or meetings without requiring a generic feed.

## Method

1. Ask participating members to answer a small number of optional personality prompts.
2. Let each member pin the answers or stories they believe represent them.
3. Show the content only in approved profile and event contexts.
4. Let a member privately shortlist and approach someone with a specific evidence-based
   conversation starter.
5. Compare relevant funnel outcomes with members or profiles that lack personality content.
6. Interview both groups about authenticity, confidence, privacy, pressure, and usefulness.

## Candidate prompt themes

- Where someone is usually found after work.
- What they have been unusually excited about.
- Their ideal Saturday afternoon.
- How close friends describe them.
- An activity for which they want a partner-in-crime.
- A quality they appreciate in another person.
- Something they take surprisingly seriously outside work.

The final prompt set and prohibited categories must be frozen before the experiment.

## Required controls

- Optional participation and explicit visibility defaults.
- Edit, delete, unpin, hide, report, and recommendation-exclusion controls.
- Event-attendee access rule.
- Explicit opt-out from Approach Copilot use.
- Moderation owner and response target.
- Prohibited sensitive and intimate prompt categories.
- No AI-generated content published without member review.

## Measures

### Primary

- Profile view → shortlist conversion with and without personality content.
- Reply rate when an approach cites a specific approved prompt or story.
- Mutual-interest and confirmed-meeting conversion.

### Supporting

- Personality-assisted approach rate.
- Member-reported confidence and conversation specificity.

### Guardrails

- Hide, report, deletion, and recommendation-exclusion.
- Sensitive-content incidents.
- Perceived pressure to perform or disclose more than intended.
- Incorrect or unwanted use as recommendation evidence.

## Thresholds

Quantitative Continue / Iterate / Stop thresholds are **TBD** and must be frozen before exposure.
Posting, reactions, impressions, and session time are not success thresholds.

## Result

Not started.

## Decision

Proceed to a broader personality surface only if the content improves selection, conversation,
mutual interest, or meetings without unacceptable privacy, safety, or performance pressure.
Success does not authorize a generic feed.
